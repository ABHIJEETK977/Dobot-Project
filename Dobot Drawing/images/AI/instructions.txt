Promt1
"Create a minimalist, simplified black and white line art sketch from the attached image.
This image is for an algorithmic vector tracing process, so it must have these exact characteristics:

Pure Black & White: Consist only of solid black lines on a pure white background.
No Shading or Texture: Absolutely no gradients, halftones, or shading.
Essential Lines Only: Reduce the subject to its absolute core structural lines and major contours (like the outline of the head, body, and main features).
No Fine Details: Explicitly exclude small details like individual hair strands, wrinkles, or background textures.
The goal is a clean, simple, high-contrast outline that a trace algorithm can convert to a few, smooth vector paths."

Prompt3
Convert the portrait into sparse robotic-trace pen line art for vector tracing.

Hard constraints:
- Pure white background.
- Thin black contour lines only.
- No filled black regions anywhere.
- No stencil look, no silhouette look.
- No shading, hatching, gradients, or texture.
- Keep the drawing minimal but recognizable.

Hair:
- No solid hair fill.
- Show hair only as the outer hairstyle boundary plus 3 to 8 major flow lines.
- Leave the hair interior mostly white.

Eyebrows and facial hair:
- No solid eyebrow fill.
- No solid beard or moustache fill.
- Facial hair, if present, must be reduced to a few contour strokes only.

Face:
- Preserve essential facial structure: face outline, jawline, eyes, nose, lips, and smile.

Smile and teeth:
- If the source portrait shows visible teeth, retain the teeth in simplified form.
- Teeth must remain white.
- Do not draw every tooth individually.
- Indicate the teeth using a light outer contour of the teeth region and only a few minimal separator lines if necessary.
- Do not convert the mouth interior into a dark filled opening unless clearly required by the reference.

Output style:
- Clean pen-outline drawing.
- Structurally readable.
- Suitable for robotic tracing and vector path extraction.
